About

Hi, I'm Julia — a marketer with a designer's eye and an architect's patience.

Portrait placeholder

I'm a brand & content designer based in New York, working where marketing strategy meets visual craft. I like the part of the job where a fuzzy idea becomes a system — a palette, a voice, a grid, a content calendar — and suddenly a brand feels like a place you'd actually want to spend time.

I started in architecture before finding my way into marketing, and that training never really left. I think in structure and proportion, but I lead with culture, story, and taste. I've shaped brand identities, built social-first campaigns, art-directed photography, and sweated the details on everything from decks to merch.

Somewhere in there I lived in Australia, fell hard for interiors, and developed a slightly unreasonable relationship with moodboards. I'm endlessly curious, genuinely collaborative, and at my best when there's a team, a deadline, and a brand worth caring about.
