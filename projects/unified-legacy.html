03 — Brand Identity

Unified Legacy

A calm, credible identity for Unified Legacy Advisors — a financial brand that needed to feel as trustworthy in print as the advice behind it.

Role
Brand Designer (via Triad)
Timeline
4 weeks
Discipline
Identity · Print
Deliverables
Logo · Stationery · System

Overview

Working with Triad Partners, I helped shape an identity for Unified Legacy Advisors — a wealth brand where trust is the entire product. The system had to feel established without feeling stuffy, modern without chasing trends.

I focused on a restrained palette, a confident wordmark, and stationery that signals care in the details — the kind of polish clients read as competence.

The Challenge

Earn trust at a glance.

Financial brands live or die on credibility. The challenge was an identity that feels steady and human — premium enough to reassure, warm enough to approach.

My role: I contributed identity design and the print system — logo refinement, type, color, and stationery — within Triad's brand strategy.
