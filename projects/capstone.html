Overview

My capstone is the strategy side of how I work — the thinking that happens before a single pixel. I ran the research, mapped the competitive landscape, and translated it into a positioning a brand could actually build on.

The perceptual map is the centerpiece: a simple visual that makes a complex market legible and points to the white space worth owning.

The Challenge

Turn data into a decision.

Research is only useful if it leads somewhere. The challenge was synthesizing a crowded market into one clear point of view — and a recommendation a team could rally behind.

My role: I led research, competitive analysis, the perceptual mapping, and the final positioning & recommendation.
